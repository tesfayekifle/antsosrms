About our Academy

Antsokiya Heywot Berhan Academy has been a beacon of educational excellence since its transformation in 2013 E.C., when it was acquired from its former identity under Sister Tezeru. Originally established in 2000 E.C., the institution began its journey with a vision to serve the local community through quality education. With a renewed name and mission, Antsokiya Heywot Berhan Academy has since grown into a respected center of learning and development. We proudly serve students from Nursery through Grade 8, offering a well-rounded curriculum that nurtures academic strength, moral values, creativity, and social responsibility.

Our Achievements

🏆 Ranked 1st in the Sidama Region level in 2015 and 2016 E.C.—a testament to our academic rigor and outstanding performance.
🎓 Our students consistently demonstrate excellence in national and local assessments.

🌱 What Makes Us Special

📚 A holistic curriculum integrating academics, arts, technology, and moral education.

🤝 Strong school-community partnership and parental involvement.
🏫 A safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for every child and Focus on character-building, leadership, discipline, and lifelong learning.

Where to Find Us
